ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Oluwo Ifeoluwa is a graduate of Tai Solarin University of Education, Ijagun, Ijebu Ode, Ogun State where she studied Agricultural Science. She served under the Mountain of Fire and Miracle Ministry’s Campus Fellowship as the Financial Secretary of the Church twice.

ABOUT THE BOOK

A patriarch of the Faith was passing through a certain village some years ago, asking if anyone was dead, and he searched the entire town for anyone dead to be brought back to life. Fortunately, he met a family whose child had just died, and the child who had died had come back to life. If something is declared dead, it can no longer live in its natural habitat. It makes no difference how dead you are; Lazarus was dead for four days, even decaying, but when Jesus Christ arrived, he called him forth from the grave. If you're reading this and you're dead in your spirit, body, and mind, all you need is Jesus Christ to wake you up.
